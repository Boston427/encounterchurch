Mission

Love God, Love People and lead them to become fully devoted followers of Jesus

Vision

A church for anybody and everybody to come as they are

God's Word

The Bible is our authority and we desire to build our lives on its truths.

Relational Evangelism

Life is about relationships and we intentionally engage with people, where they are, to communicate God's love.

Authentic Community

We desire a community where people are transformed by the gospel and can develop authentic relationships. We want to live life on life!

Irrational Generosity

We believe in giving sacrificially to the work of God in our city and in the world. You cannot out give God! We will lead the way through irrational generosity!

Our Pastors

Will Zik -- Lead Pastor

Will is the Lead Pastor of Encounter Community. Will, along with several others, had a vision to take a leap of faith by following God's leadership and begin Encounter Community. Will has a heart for the city of Owensboro, the state of Kentucky, and the world. His passion is to meet people where they are, see them changed by the love of God through a relationship with Jesus Christ, and to help them become all Christ wants them to become through discipleship. Will and his wife, Brittany, have two sons: Liam and Tytus.

Parvin Phillips -- Pastoral Care Pastor

Parvin is truly a man after God's own heart. For years he has been serving the Lord and has a strong desire to make the Gospel known in all the world. He has a heart for broken people and to see them experience life change. Parvin, along with his wife Sheila, have a life long mission to love people, engage them where they are, and to make disciples who make disciples.
